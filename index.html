My Story

I am Prarthi Jain, a diligent and innovative professional with a rich blend of educational and work experiences spread across India and the USA. Having pursued a Master of Engineering in Computer Science from Cornell University, followed by a Master of Science (Research) in Computer Science Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Indore, I have honed my technical and analytical abilities. My professional journey has taken me from Cloudera in Bangalore, India, where I contributed as a Software Engineer, to Dexterity, Inc in Redwood City, California, where I currently thrive as a Software Engineer 2. At Dexterity, I am part of the data team, focusing on building APIs for real-time data monitoring from robots, which showcases my ability to adapt and contribute to cutting-edge technology landscapes. My research pursuits in anomaly detection and energy-efficient fault detection in constrained environments reflect my penchant for delving deep into complex problems and coming up with viable solutions. Besides my technical endeavors, I am also an active participant in community service, being a part of NGOs like HOPE and AVANA, organizing and participating in events that make a positive impact in the community. Through these experiences, I have not only expanded my technical horizon but also honed my ability to collaborate, innovate, and make meaningful contributions wherever I set my foot.
